This Privacy Policy explains how Market Video Automation handles and protects your information.

  • 透過 TikTok OAuth 2.0 回傳的授權憑證（Authorization Code / Access Token）

Information is used solely to perform authorized video uploads and not for marketing or advertising.

4. 資料儲存與安全 / Data Storage and Security

存取權杖會以加密方式儲存，僅於執行自動化任務時短暫使用。

Access tokens are stored securely and used only during automated execution.

5. 資料保留與刪除 / Data Retention and Deletion

若您撤銷 TikTok 授權或停止使用本服務，相關存取權杖將立即失效並刪除。

When authorization is revoked or service usage stops, access tokens are promptly deleted.

The Service interacts only with official TikTok APIs and complies with TikTok platform policies.
